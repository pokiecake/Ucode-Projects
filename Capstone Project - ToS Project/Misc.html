Achievements

Each role and faction has achievements associated with them. Each role has 4 of the same achievements. The achievements are winning with that role 1, 5, 10, and 25 times. The rest are specific to that role. When you satisfy the conditions of the achievement, you get an amount of Merit points depending on the achievement. The faction achievements are usually winning with half and all of the roles in the faction. The town faction and Mafia faction also have an achievement of having all of the roles in their faction alive.

Avatar

Your avatar is what you will look like when you enter a game of ToS. The store has skins, houses, pets, death animations, and more for your avatar. These items need to be bought by Town points or merit points (Sometimes Town points only, sometimes Town points or merit points). You are only able to display one of each item, or just have no pet or death animation. Many default skins and houses are given to you, but you have to buy/get Pets and death animations (the default death animation is the hanging animation).

Forums

The town of Salem forums are where people can make an account, talk about various subjects, get the updates or news about ToS, and many more things. Signing up as a user on the forums is free. You can see a community and talk with the staff members at Blankmediagames. Another thing you can do is post your ideas for a new role to put into ToS. You can post your role idea or review others to decide if the role should be in ToS.

Victories and draws

At the end of the game, there will be a victory or draw. A draw happens when there have been no kills in a certain day, everyone has died (with no jester or executioner win), or the remaining players cannot kill another by attack or lynch. With the third condition, the game may give an auto win to prevent draws like that. The win will go to the faction with the lowest winrate (Arsonist, Werewolf, Serial Killer, Mafia, Town). The stale mate detector also gives the win to the faction that would already win in a 1v1 situation. The current victories are the Town, Mafia, Coven, and all neutral roles have their own victory except Amnesiac. Some roles can win without their victory message being achieved because one of the bigger factions succeeded (roles like the witch, survivor, and jester) There is also a Neutrals victory, only possible when a jester and an executioner achieved their goal without any other faction winning.
