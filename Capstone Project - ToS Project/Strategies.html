Note: These strategies won't get you to win every game. These are strategies are mainly from expierence and the Town of Salem Wikia. Coven roles are omitted from this page as I don't have the Coven DLC.

Town

Town has to kill any evildoers*. They have to use their various abilities to find and kill evildoers. They have to watch out for jesters, as jesters are more of a threat to town than any other role. A survivor may also betray them in the future.

Anyone who is too quiet might be evil and should require visits by the Jailor or investigative roles to make sure they aren't evil. Unless the jester will harm the town, you shouldn't execute them as a jailor since it will waste an execution. A vigilante would be more appropiate. The role that can figure out the Mafia and Coven roles is the Spy. The spy sees who the Mafia and/or Coven visit. If there aren't many visits (apart from killing visits), then the Mafia likely has forger and janitor (this is also supported more if the spy sees 2 visits to the same dead person). If there are 2 or 3 visits every day, then it might have Consigliere, Consort, Framer, or other roles. (If a person claims roleblocked and a Mafia member visited them, there is most likely a consort.) The Jailor can also jail and demand a claim from anyone. The Jailor can deal 3 unstoppable attacks to 3 targets. Anyone who is too quiet or doesn't respond right away may be evil.

Mafia

The Mafia has to kill anyone who doesn't submit to the mafia*. They use their abilities to kill and deceive the town as well as other roles. Only the Godfather and Mafioso chooses who to kill, and the mafioso has to kill whoever the Godfather chooses unless the Godfather is unavaliable. All Mafia roles should think of roles to fake-claim. Consigliere's and Consorts can easily claim Investigator and Escort respectivly. The biggest threat to Mafia is the spy, as the spy can see whoever the Mafia visits. This means that the spy will see who the Consigliere and Consort visit, meaning that they can use the Mafia visits to say that the Consigliere and Consort are guilty. Blackmailer can see whispers and prevent a person from talking. Blackmailer and Consigliere should report any info to the Mafia chat, as they can't communicate to the Mafia when they are dead. The biggest threat to Mafia are Neutral Killings since the Mafia can't kill Neutral Killings. Even if they find the neutral Killing, they shouldn't out them right away, as the Neutral Killing can kill people the Mafia don't have to.

Neutral Killing

Neutral Killing roles have to kill anyone who opposes them*. They are the lone wolves. The serial killer can kill every night, but are generally weak. A sheriff is also able to find a serial killer. The werewolf can kill during Full moons and will kill their target and anyone who visits their target. They can only be found by a sheriff on full moon nights. Also, if the serial killer and a werewolf on a full moon night is jailed and not executed, they will kill the jailor. The arsonist kills people slowly, but has the highest attack. They douse people each night, and can choose to kill everyone they douse by igniting them. They should kill active players, as the less active players could be evils or neutrals that can help them. In Normal Games, they will be the only neutral killing. This means that they won't have to watch for other Neutral Killings and vampires. Arsonist can technically claim any role, as dousing someone will change their investigative results. So the Arsonist can claim doused. The werewolf should target people who claim TP (make sure there's no bodyguard, or else the werewolf will die) to leave the town vulnerable. They can also kill quiet people, as TI's might target them. The serial killer should kill active people, as they can be people who will find the serial killer.

Neutral Evil

Jesters should be suspicious, but not too suspicious. Being quiet may work, but you might get a visit from the jailor or get shot by a vigilante instead. When they are dead, it might be a good idea to claim and try to mislead the town. Claiming a role, and providing wrong information can work, especially if the town goes along with you. Acting too suspicious will make people believe that you are a jester. Executioners can follow a similar strategy by claiming a role and putting suspicioun on their target. The witch has to find the Mafia or Neutral Killing. They also have to find the Town roles, especially jailor, so they know what they can fake-claim and to use the correct abilities on the Town or Evils.

Neutral Benign

The survivor, while winning with every role, isn't easy to win with. The Town will distrust survivors if they don't contribute with finding evils. The Mafia and Neutral Killings might want to kill the Survivors since they can turn on them and lynch then. Survivor is also a claim that evils fake-claim, putting more distrust on Survivors. Survivors should side with the faction that will win. This will mean that the faction they side with won't kill them, although you can also betray them and should betray if they turn into the losing faction. The Amnesiac is similar to Survivor, as it should remember the role that is winning. It can also remember early and take sides. The disadvantage is that you can't claim Amnesiac anymore. The Guardian Angel has to predict wether or not their target will get attacked. They can win as a survivor, but it will be harder than a regular survivor as they don't have any vests.

Neutral Chaos

Vampires have to choose who to convert. They have to watch out for Vampire hunters, as they are the only role who can find vampires. The vampire hunter can hear vampire chat, so the vampires shouldn't type the way they would type during the day. Otherwise, the Vampire hunter can link up the vampires' typing to a person's typing during the day. When the vampires have 4 members, they can deal a basic attack to someone. They should probably kill the same people a serial killer would kill, active people and investigatives. The pirate has to choose carefully who to duel, although that part isn't too important. The longer the game lasts, the better the chance they can get 2 plunders. Go to the roles page to learn its mechanics. The common tracker claim for Plaguebearer is tracker, since the Plaguebearer knows who is infected. If an infected player visits another player, they will become infected. This is the main reason why plaguebearers claim tracker. When they become pestilence, they are top priority. They should become Pestilence when there are few players left alive. Tracker claims will be more sus if they aren't confirmed. People won't be likely to visit, so you can only kill one person per night. You should be talkative and provide the Town with leads on who could be Pestilence.

Goals*

Every role has a goal. The town has to kill evildoers, who are the Mafia, Neutral Killing, Neutral Chaos except Pirate, and Coven. The Mafia has to kill anyone who doesn't submit to the Mafia, who are the Town, Neutal Killing, Neutral Chaos except Pirate, and coven. Each Neutral Killing wins with the same Neutral Killing role, Neutral Benign, Neutral Evil, and pirate. The jester has to get lynched, the executioner has to lynch their target, the witch has to survive and kill the town, the survivor wins with everybody, the Amnesiac has to remember a role and win with their goal, and the Guardian Angel wins when their target survives until the end of the game. The vampire has to kill the Town, Mafia, Neutral Killing, Plaguebearer/Pestilence, and Coven. The plaguebearer only wins with Neutral Benign, Neutral Evil, and Pirate. Go to the Roles page for more information.
