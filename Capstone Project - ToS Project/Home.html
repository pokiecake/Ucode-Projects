Factions

In ToS, there are 2 main factions. They are the Town and the Mafia. The town do not know who eachother are, and they are trying to get rid of the mafia using their abilities. The Mafia know who eachother are, and there can be as much as four members of the Mafia at a time. They are able to kill one person every night and also have abilities to help the Mafia and deceive the town. There is another faction lurking about called the Neutrals. The neutrals have their own unique goals and have their own unique abilities. While some can win with the Mafia and/or Town, some win by themselves.

Roles

Everyone is assigned a role before the game starts. All the roles have an alignment, which is the summary of the role. Some town roles are town investigative. They use their abilities to investigate others to find out if they are town or the mafia. There is also town killing. They use their abilities to kill any evildoers, but they can also kill town members if they mess up. Another alignment Neutral killing, which win by themselves and any neutrals whose goals do not counteract with their goals. There are a lot of roles that are fun to play, and some roles are harder to win than others.

Store and Currency

In ToS, there are 2 units of Currency called Merit points and Town points. Merit points are earned by playing games, whether or not you won or lost. Town points were like merit points, but after version 1.5.9 of Town of salem, they need to be purchased using real money. You can also get Town points from the daily brew. You can use Merit and Town points in the store to buy avatars, death animations, pets, and other items. Some items can only be bought by town points, and some items don't use Merit or Town points, but certain goals to unlock them. Once you use something from the store, it will show up in your next game of Town of Salem, and you can also you your item if it is a consumable.

Daily Brew

The daily brew is a system that gives you rewards. Every day, you can go to the daily brew and pour a potion into the cauldron. Everytime you pour a potion in, you will get 20 merit points. It takes 7 potions in order to get a reward, meaning it will take 7 days to get the reward. The reward is anything from the shop excluding bundles or goal specific items. You can also charm your reward with a variety of potions that the Daily brew gives you. They all cost town points, but it can give you great rewards. If you already own an item, it will not appear as a reward for the Daily brew with the exception of taunts and scrolls if you don't have the max of a type of scroll.
