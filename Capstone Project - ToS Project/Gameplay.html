Day and Night cycles

In ToS, the game cycles to day and night. During the day, talk with the other townspeople to find out who the evil doers are. Anyone can make accusations against other players, and you are able to vote on who you think is guilty. You are also to claim your role during the day, but some people, especially evildoers, will lie about their role to try to not look guilty. During the night, you are able to use your role's ability, though some people have day abilities, and some people have day and night abilities. The abilities range from being able to kill someone, to finding out whether or not someone is evil, or even jailing someone and being able to execute them. All of the role's abilities are unique, and it will be a fun experience playing ToS.

Last Wills

All of the roles have last wills. Last wills allow you to write your notes. If you are an investigative role, you can write your findings down. However, if you are evil, then you will have to think of a fake claim to fool the town. If you are suspicious, people will usually ask you to copy and paste a will. Your last will will be visible to everyone when you die, with a few exceptions.

Death

Death can happen in many ways. From being shot by the mafia to suicide, there are a lot of ways to die. Whenever someone dies, their last will is shown. They also enter the death chat, which is this chat where they can talk to the other dead people, and can see the day chat and night chats if they have one. No one can see the death chats apart from the medium and anyone who they seance. The only way to get alive again is to be a town role and have the Retributionist revive you.

Voting and Treason

During the day phase, there is also a phase called the Discussion and Voting phase. The discussion phase allows you to talk with everyone and talk about findings and who they think is evil. The Voting phase is when people vote for the person they think is evil. In order to get on the stand, they must have enough votes, which is half the number of players plus one. After the player gets on the stand, they have some time to defend themselves. Then everyone excluding the player and the dead vote guilty, innocent, or abstain. If there is a majority innocent votes, or if there's a tie, the player gets pardoned off the stand and people enter the voting phase again (There is a maximum of three pardonings, then the town enters the night phase). If there is a majority of guilty votes, then the player gets executed, with 5 seconds to say their last words. Then their will is shown, then their role is revealed. After that the town enters the night phase or the game ends if everyone's goal is the same.

Game modes - Normal

There are many game modes in Town of Salem. Each gamemodes are divided into sections. The first section is the Normal section, holding the Classic, Ranked Practice, and Ranked game modes. Classic is the first classic game modes New people can play. This holds less random roles, generally for new players to learn the basics of ToS. The Ranked practice game mode is unlocked when new players play 10 game modes of ToS. This game mode is also played by ranked players. Ranked is unlocked by playing 50 games of ToS, 10 of them being Ranked practice. Ranked and Ranked practice has the same role list, but ranked practice, like the name, is to practice for Ranked. When you play Ranked, you won't know who you are up against at all, and no scrolls will be used. Ranked is also different than Ranked practice because Ranked allows the players to play in seasons. Basically, you are able to gain or lose elo when playing Ranked, unless it is your first 10 games in Ranked. The more elo you get, the more expierence players you will play against. Once the Seasons of ranked are over, your elo is reset, and You will get rewards depending on the number of elo you got.

Other Gamemodes

The other 2 sections are the Custom and Chaos sections. The Custom gamemodes are the Custom and Rapid gamemodes. The Chaos game modes are the All Any, Rainbow, and dracula's palace gamemodes. The custom game mode allows the host (the first person to join the lobby, or randomly selected if enough people want to repick the host) to make up their own role list. It can be anything, and they can start with at least 7 people in the lobby. The rapid game mode is like Custom, but the Discussion and night phase timer are cut in half. In the chaos section, the All any game mode makes the role list Any, meaning anyone can be any role. The rainbow Gamemode role list is the Godfather, Arsonist, Survivor, Jailor, Amnesiac, Serial killer, Witch, then it goes backward in the first 7 roles (witch, Serial killer, etc.) replacing Jailor with Veteran, Godfather with Mafioso, and has the Any role in the middle. The roles' color creates a rainbow. Finally, the last game mode is the Dracula's palace game mode. It has 4 starting vampires and 1 vampire hunter. The vampires are able to kill every night as they have a full team, but can only convert when they have 3 or less members. These are the remaining game modes.
