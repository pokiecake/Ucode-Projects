What it is

Not to be confused with club penguin Island, this is a game where you can hang out with other penguins and have fun with games or parties. If you're new, you can have a tour of the place to better understand it. This page will talk about the features generally.

Events and Parties

Throughout the year, CPR will have events and parties. Some include a New Year's party, Operation Blackout, and the Water Party. If you want to see a list of parties, go here

Game play and socializing

There are a bunch of games that you are able to play in CPR. You can earn coins in all of the games. With the coins, you can buy clothing, items, furniture, etc. Plus, you can socialize with other penguins and assemble a group to do something like play a band or play a hockey team.

Rooms and pins

There are a lot of rooms in CPR. Some rooms are only accessible during events. You will find a different environment in all of them, and you might remember the names of all of the rooms. You can find mini-games, party rooms, and pins. Pins are scattered everywhere and expire at some date. You can put pins on your player cards. Pins are usually found in rooms, hidden.

Coins

Coins are the currency in the game. You earn coins by entering a code, mining, or playing mini-games. Once you earn your coins, you can spend them in various ways. You can buy clothing, furniture, or backgrounds. You will probably want or need items so you have to earn your coins to get it. You will most likely get them from mini-games.
