The legends of St.Nicholas.

St. Nicholas is a guy who loves children and gives gifts. He was raised as a christan as a child, but his parents died in an epidemic while he was still young. He then later became a bishop of Myra and would use his money to give to the sick, needy, and poor. He was known for his generosity and his love for children.

Spread the word of St.Nick and have a wonderful Christmas!
