Polynomial plug-in. Yay!

In the constants input, enter the coefficents seperated by spaces.
e.g. x^4 + 4x^3 - 5x^2 + 9x + 20 -> 1 4 -5 9 20.

The number input is what you're plugging in the x with. Fractions are allowed in the inputs.
